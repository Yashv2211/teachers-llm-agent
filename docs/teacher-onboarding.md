# Welcome to Nuru

Give every student a personal tutor — free, forever.

---

## Get started in 3 steps

**Step 1 — Sign up**
Go to your Nuru link and sign in with your email address. You'll receive a one-time code — enter it to get in.

**Step 2 — Create your first tutor**
Tap **New Agent** and give it a name (e.g. "Chapter 4 Verbs Helper"). Choose your subject, grade level, and language. Paste in your teaching instructions, or upload a PDF of your curriculum notes to ground the tutor in your actual material. Tap **Create Agent**.

**Step 3 — Share with your students**
On the agent page you'll see a QR code. Show it on the board or print it out. Students scan it with their phone camera — no app download, no account needed. They speak their question and hear the answer in their language.

---

## Tips

- **Upload your notes.** The more context you give the tutor, the more accurate it is. A PDF of your lesson plan works perfectly.
- **It works offline-ish.** The student interface loads on weak connections. The AI responses need internet, but the page itself is fast.
- **Questions are logged.** Check the agent page after class to see what your students asked — useful for spotting gaps.
